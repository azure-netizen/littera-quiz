# Analytics and Reporting Requirements

## Purpose

Provide performance, engagement, participation, and quality reports for students, teachers, and administrators.

## Users

- Student
- Teacher
- Admin

## Functional Requirements

- Student analytics shall include performance trends.
- Student analytics shall include accuracy reports.
- Student analytics shall include rank progression.
- Teacher analytics shall include student onboarding count.
- Teacher analytics shall include question approval ratio.
- Teacher analytics shall include leaderboard rank tracking.
- Admin analytics shall include daily active users.
- Admin analytics shall include engagement rate.
- Admin analytics shall include quiz completion rate.
- Admin analytics shall include teacher participation rate.
- Reports shall support hierarchy-based filtering where applicable.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Students can view their own performance and rank progression.
- Teachers can view performance for enrolled students.
- Teachers can view contribution and approval metrics.
- Admins can view platform-level success metrics.

## Dependencies

- Gameplay results
- Leaderboards
- Question approval workflow
- User hierarchy data
