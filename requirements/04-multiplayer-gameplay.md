# Multiplayer Gameplay Requirements

## Purpose

Enable students to compete in real-time formats including 1v1 battles, group competitions, tournaments, timed challenges, and cooperative gameplay.

## Users

- Student
- Admin

## Functional Requirements

- The student can also select questions from a category and sub category or all or two/three category together.
- The system shall support 1v1 quiz battles. In this  student can give challange to  other students by sending chanllange request. 
- Challange will give additional reward points winning the battel.
- The system shall support group competitions.
- The system shall support tournament mode.
- The system shall support timed challenges.
- The system shall support cooperative gameplay where configured.
- The system shall match players based on configured competition rules.
- The system shall display live opponent movement and ranking.
- The system shall handle player disconnection and reconnection.
- The system shall publish match results after completion.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A student can join a 1v1 match.
- A student can join a group competition.
- Tournament rounds progress according to configured rules.
- Live position and score updates are visible during gameplay.
- Match results update relevant leaderboards.

## Dependencies

- Real-time system
- Slot participation
- Leaderboard system
- Question bank
