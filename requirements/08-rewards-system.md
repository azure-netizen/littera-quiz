# Rewards System Requirements

## Purpose

Provide certificates, badges, and incentives for top-performing students and teachers through a configurable reward engine.

## Users

- Student
- Teacher
- Admin

## Functional Requirements

- The system shall issue certificates.
- The system shall issue achievement badges.
- The system shall support incentives for top students.
- The system shall support incentives for top teachers.
- Admins shall be able to configure reward rules.
- Rewards shall be linked to quiz performance, leaderboard rank, participation, or contribution.
- Users shall be able to view earned rewards.
- Users shall be able to share eligible rewards.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A student receives a certificate after meeting completion criteria.
- A teacher receives a badge after meeting contribution criteria.
- Admin-configured reward rules determine eligibility.
- Rewards are visible in user profiles.

## Dependencies

- Leaderboards
- Teacher scoring
- Student performance data
- Social sharing
