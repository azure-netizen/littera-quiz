# Customization Requirements

## Purpose

Allow the platform to support quiz themes, difficulty levels, and category-based quiz experiences.

## Users

- Admin
- Teacher
- Student

## Functional Requirements

- The system shall support quiz themes.
- The system shall support difficulty-level configuration.
- The system shall support category-based quizzes.
- Admins shall be able to configure available categories.
- Admins shall be able to configure game and quiz settings.
- Students shall see quiz experiences based on selected or assigned themes and categories.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Admin can create or update quiz categories.
- A quiz can be filtered by difficulty and category.
- The student experience reflects selected theme and category configuration.

## Dependencies

- Question bank
- Admin control center
- Game mechanics
